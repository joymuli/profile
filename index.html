Joy Muli

Profile

Joy is a passionate designer from Nairobi, Kenya. She graduated with honours with a Bachelo's degree in Design from the University of Nairobi. She has previously worked for a number of companies in Kenya as a graphic designer and nterior designerand was the winner of an interior design competition organised by The Centre for Biotechnology and Bioinformatics at The University of Nairobi. She was also 2nd runners up in the African Fund for Endangered Wildlife Art Competition. Joy has taken part in various design thinking workshops including the Nairobi Design Week.

As a 10 Academy Fellow, Joy has gained training in Modern Skills; Design thinking, Making, 3D printing, Mathematics and Physics, Entrepreneurship and Management, Economics, Data and Society, Communications, Software Development.
